Get in touch

You talkin' to me?

I wish it wasn't this way (working in a team is more fun), but the block'em project is driven and maintained only by me, Faustino Frechilla: I am the designer and the developer of this whole thing.

I will be glad to hear any comments about block'em (either good or bad): bug reports, feature requests or just plain greetings. So, if you feel like it, just drop me an e-mail.

Alternatively to the old e-mail way, you can submit your bug or feature request in sourceforge. Either way your messages will get to me.

Bear in mind though, that this project is done in my spare time, which means that there may be an undetermined delay in my responses. Do not take it as an offence, I really appreciate that you decided to contact me, but sometimes my spare time is not as long as I wish. What I'm saying is that I can't promise I'll answer you right away, but I will get back to you.
